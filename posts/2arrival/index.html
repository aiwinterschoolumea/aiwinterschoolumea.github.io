Arrival to venue

The Responsible ML Winter School will take place in the MIT-Building of Ume책 University. This is located close to the Universum Bus stop. A parking lot of the university is located close to the Universum. By public transport, the best way to reach the university from the City centre is to take bus lines 2, 5, 8 or 9 to the bus stop Universum (travel time about 10 minutes). If you like to walk, then it’ll take around 25 minutes.
